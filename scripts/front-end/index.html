Unit Conversion Validation

Input and Target UOMs should either be volume or temperature UOMs. The following values are valid:

Temperature

  • kelvin
  • celsius
  • fahrenheit
  • rankine

Volume

  • liters
  • tablespoons
  • cubic inches
  • cups
  • cubic feet
  • gallons

Enter the value and UOM to convert from, the UOM to convert to, and your guess for what the result should be.
